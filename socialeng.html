What is phishing?

A malicious entity contacts you, masquerading as a legitimate business and asks for information, directs you to a false website that tries to obtain your information, or otherwise tries to rip you off and exploit you.

Phishing Warning Signs
  • Requests for personal information
  • Generic greeting
  • Urgency
  • False links
Fake Websites

Phishing emails can take you to fake websites that are designed to look like legitimate websites.
